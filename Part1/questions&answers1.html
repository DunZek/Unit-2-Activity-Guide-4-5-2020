2. Brainstorm and record: List 3 ways in which an educated population is both in people's personal interest and in the national interest.

  • Educated population:
    • Personal interest:
      • Capable to be competitive at work.
      • Improvement of quality of life due to the work of specialized individuals.
      • Your company can benefit.
    • National interest:
      • The entire nation might develop slower due to the lack of talented individuals.
      • Economic growth is usually controlled by powerful company leaders, whom of which are usually the smartest individuals of the nation.
      • Thus in the end, an uneducated population may slow down a nation's progress in pursuing their goals.

3. Write two definitions for each of the following (use your text for one definition and use another source for your second definition).

  • Foreign policy:
    • A plan of action that guides a government's decisions about its official relations with other countries . Also called foreign affairs or external relations.
    • Google: A government's strategy in dealing with other nations.
  • Domestic policy:
    • A plan of action that guides a government's decisions about what to do within a country. In Canada, domestic policy decisions may include changing federal laws, settling indigenous land claims, and spending tax revenues.
    • Cambridge dictionary: The set of decisions that a government makes relating to things that directly affect the people in its own country.
